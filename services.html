Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Explore FAQs about our Kerala-wide digital displays on KSRTC bus stands. Uncover advertising benefits, content options, and launch impactful campaigns with ease.

  • How can I book slots for displaying my video ads in KSRTC buses?

    You can book slots for displaying your video ads in KSRTC buses by contacting our advertising team. We offer limited slots, so it's essential to act quickly to secure your advertising space.

  • What is the minimum duration for displaying video ads in KSRTC buses?

    The minimum duration for displaying video ads is one day. However, you have the option to extend your advertising campaign for up to 50 or more times throughout the month to maximize visibility.

  • How do I know when and how often my video ads are displayed?

    We provide real-time reporting, allowing you to track the frequency and timing of your video ads' display. You'll have access to detailed insights on the performance of your advertising campaign.

  • Can I advertise multiple brands using this service?

    Yes, you can advertise multiple brands. You have the flexibility to rotate and switch ads, making it easy to target different demographics effectively.

  • What kind of screens are used for displaying video ads in KSRTC buses?

    We use 43-inch Full HD screens, ensuring that your video ads are displayed in high clarity, attracting more viewers and maximizing the impact of your advertising campaign.

  • How many people use KSRTC buses daily?

    KSRTC buses transport more than 2 crore (20 million) people daily, providing a massive reach for your brand to connect with a vast audience in Kerala.
